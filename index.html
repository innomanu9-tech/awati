Our mission

Practical innovation for women-led agriculture.

From precision irrigation advice to access to finance and community-led training, AWATI builds pathways that turn opportunity into measurable impact.

Climate-smart farming

We teach drought-tolerant cropping, soil health practices, and sustainable inputs that help families protect yields.

Digital training

Our mobile-first learning programs share weather alerts, market pricing, and best practices directly to women farmers.

Community leadership

We develop local champions who mentor peer groups, manage cooperatives, and shape inclusive food networks.
