About AWATI

We believe women are central to Africa’s food future.

AWATI is a nonprofit focused on strengthening women-led food systems through training, digital tools, and long-term partnerships.

Featured video

Women Leading the Future of African Agriculture

Our story

From community insight to scalable change.

We started with a simple belief: when women have equal access to knowledge, technology, and opportunity, agriculture becomes more productive and more just. Today, our programs support women farmers, processors, agripreneurs, and community educators across the continent.

Our principles

  • Evidence-based and field-tested support
  • Inclusive engagement for rural communities
  • Transparent stewardship of every donation

What drives us

Three connected pillars that sustain our work.

Economic opportunity

We connect women to finance, markets, and training that expand earning potential.

Climate resilience

We help families adapt to changing environments through regenerative and practical farming.

Leadership development

We develop local leaders capable of guiding cooperatives, peer training, and public engagement.

More about AWATI

See our mission, vision, and objectives.

Mission

We empower women-led agricultural communities across Africa with training, technology, and access to markets.

Vision

A future where women farmers lead sustainable food systems that nourish families, economies, and ecosystems.

Objective

To build long-term partnerships and tools that improve productivity, resilience, and financial independence for women in agriculture.

🌱 AWATI

Empowering African women to lead resilient agri-tech systems through knowledge, finance, and community action.
