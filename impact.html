How we measure success

Data, stories, and field learning guide our next steps.

Income stability

We monitor changes in earnings, savings habits, and business resilience over time.

Food security

We evaluate household access to nutritious food and the quality of harvests season to season.

Leadership growth

We document how participants become mentors, cooperative leaders, and trainers in their own communities.
